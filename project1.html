ESCAPE/ILLUSION - LD Jam 11

Project Breakdown

  • Length of jam: 1 month
  • Theme of Escape/Illusion
  • Created using Hammer++ in Half Life 2
  • Focused on basic execution of LD principles
  • Click here for LD Jam 11 itch page

Story Summary

What seemed like an average day at home turned out to be a recurring nightmare. Survive the trials and rescue your friend...only to start again

Overview

Gameplay beats

Top down detailed map

High-level route sketch showing stealth and puzzle crossover.

Notable Moments

False corridor reveal

A narrow passage initially reads as a safe flank path, then collapses into a hard exposure lane once the player commits.

Audio-led reroute

Guard movement audio was tuned to pull players toward a riskier shortcut, enabling emergent route planning under pressure.

Light cone inversion

A late lighting pass inverted expected danger zones so the player had to rethink previously learned stealth habits.

Final extraction sprint

The closing sequence compresses all prior systems into a timed sprint that rewards route mastery and quick adaptation.

Reflections

Goals

Create stealth tension that remains readable while introducing illusion mechanics that challenge player assumptions without feeling unfair.

Constraints

Built in Source with limited scripting bandwidth, fixed milestone deadlines, and a strict target on encounter count per development week.

Requirements

Deliver a complete vertical slice with one major stealth loop, one illusion-driven puzzle branch, and clear onboarding for first-time players.
